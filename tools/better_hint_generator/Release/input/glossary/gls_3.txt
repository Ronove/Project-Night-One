The Seven Mysteries is a
Japanese concept birthed
from the importance of the
number 7. (E.G. 7 days in a
week, 7 wonders in the world,
7 deadly sins).

This concept became popular
in the Edo period of Japanese
history (1600-1868) with tales
of unexplained phenomena that
turned into urban legends.
These tales usually took place
in a single area and comprised
of 7 phenomena.
E.G. 7 Mysteries of Suwa Shrine

This trend became popular in 
Japanese schools and seven
school mysteries is a popular
concept that has been explored
in TV dramas, anime, and manga.
